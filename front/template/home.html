Welcome to the forum ExploreUr !

This forum is for urbex enthusiasts and to provide global information and best practices

It is important to note that urbex is an illegal activity in many places. We would like to make it clear that we are in no way responsible for your actions.

However, we encourage members to share their experiences, discuss interesting places to explore and exchange tips for practicing urbex in a responsible and respectful way.

Remember to always respect private property, exercise caution, and obey the rules in your area. Safety should be the top priority during any urban exploration.

Take advantage of this forum to expand your knowledge and passion for urbex, but always remember to act responsibly and legally.

We wish you great urban discoveries and fruitful discussions within the ExpleUr community!
